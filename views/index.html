About Re-connect

Re-Connect creates an ecosystem of trust where we aggregate resources and form a support network for survivors.

 Connect the community and resources
We bring the resources in the community together to address the immediate and long-term housing needs of the survivors and connect them in a safe and supportive environment. Our ultimate goal is to put the power back in the hands of the survivors so they know where to go to ask for help and when they need them

Connect the dots
We collect data and generate insights to educate the public and bring visibility to human trafficking issues and survivor needs
